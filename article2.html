New Beauty Brand 'GlowRush' Raises Funding, Targets India's Blush & Face Glow Market

SC
Startup & Consumer Brands Desk April 19, 2025 · Bengaluru
5 min read

India's beauty and personal care segment continues to attract investor interest, with new-age cosmetics brand GlowRush announcing a fresh funding round aimed at expanding its presence in the mass-premium makeup category.

According to sources close to the development, GlowRush has raised an undisclosed amount in a pre-Series A round led by a consortium of consumer-focused investors. The brand is expected to accelerate marketing spend and product launches in the blush and face glow segment.

Industry observers believe this category has become a fast-growing segment due to influencer-driven consumer behavior and the rapid adoption of short-video commerce.

"Blush and glow products are trending heavily in 2025. Consumers are discovering makeup through reels, not through traditional ads," said an executive at a digital commerce consultancy.

GlowRush is reportedly planning an aggressive retail entry into modern trade chains and is simultaneously negotiating placements with quick commerce platforms for metro-heavy distribution.

Market analysts believe the rise of brands like GlowRush is putting pressure on established players to respond quickly with visibility campaigns, promotions, and price corrections.

"Legacy brands can't ignore the fact that these startups are grabbing attention faster. Discount-led counter-campaigns are a common response," the analyst added.
